Quick Start

  1. Open VR View on the patient's phone — note the 6-character room code.
  2. Open Controller on the doctor's phone — enter the room code and tap Connect.
  3. Place the patient's phone in the VR cardboard headset.
  4. Adjust IPD slider to match the patient. Start with Base-Out mode.
  5. Tap + to increase prism. Tap Record Break when diplopia is reported, then Record Recovery when fusion returns.
